Science is ever evolving, with today’s certainties often challenged by tomorrow’s discoveries. SIGMA 2026, themed Perplexing Paradigms, celebrates ideas and phenomena that defy expectations, driving curiosity, critical thinking, and innovation. It invites participants to question established norms, embrace uncertainty, and explore the frontiers of scientific inquiry—where every discovery opens the door to new questions and deeper understanding..
